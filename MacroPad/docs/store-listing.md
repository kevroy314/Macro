## Short Description (80 characters max)

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

**Track your macros in seconds, not minutes.**

MacroPad is the fastest way to log your daily protein, carbs, and fat intake. No complicated food databases, no barcode scanning, no subscriptions - just simple, lightning-fast macro tracking with powerful home screen widgets.

**HOME SCREEN WIDGETS**
Track your macros without even opening the app:
- Macro Status Widget: See your daily totals vs targets at a glance
- Increment Widget: Tap + or - to quickly adjust any macro
- Preset Widget: Apply your saved meals with a single tap

**PRESETS FOR SPEED**
Save your go-to meals and snacks as presets. Chicken breast? One tap. Protein shake? One tap. Your regular breakfast? One tap. Stop typing the same numbers over and over.

**FLEXIBLE DAY RESET**
Night owl? Set your tracking day to reset at 4am, 5am, or any hour that fits YOUR schedule. Your macros follow your lifestyle, not an arbitrary midnight cutoff.

**YOUR DATA, YOUR WAY**
- All data stored locally on your device
- Optional Dropbox backup for peace of mind
- Export anytime as CSV or JSON
- No account required
- No ads, no tracking, no subscriptions

**SIMPLE BY DESIGN**
MacroPad does one thing and does it well. If you know your macros and just need to track them fast, this is the app for you.

Perfect for:
- Bodybuilders and athletes tracking macros
- People following flexible dieting (IIFYM)
- Anyone who wants quick, no-fuss nutrition logging
- Users tired of bloated calorie counting apps

**OPEN SOURCE**
MacroPad is completely open source. Review the code, suggest improvements, or contribute at github.com/kevroy314/Macro
